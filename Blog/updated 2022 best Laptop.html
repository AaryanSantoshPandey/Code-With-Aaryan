[Updated 2022] Best Laptops To Buy in 2022 - Laptop Buying Guide between 40-70k INR

Hey peeps, it’s time to get back to the college in an offline mode and I hope that you all are excited and preparing yourself for it.

After going through the comments of my videos, I found there are several students who have not purchased a laptop and they’re in search of a good laptop, but confused, because there are tons of options available. Don’t worry I got you covered!

In this post, we’ll be discussing top laptops for the people whose budget is around 40-70k. There are certain points that I want to make very clear,

Firstly all the laptops contain RTX 3000 series GPU because this is the latest GPU and it has some mind-boggling features such as DLSS, Reflex, etc., and this series is based on Ampere Architecture.

1. HP Pavilion Ryzen 5600H, GeForce RTX 3050 Laptop:

This is the laptop that has driven me crazy because after looking at the specs and the price I was totally amazed. This laptop comes with Ryzen 5 5600H processor that contains 6 cores, and 12 threads. It has RTX 3050 in it and on top of that, you’ll fall in love with the design of this laptop because it looks really premium and classy laptop.

Pros:
  • It has a 15.6inch FHD Display with 144Hz and 300 nits.
  • It comes with Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth version 5.2.
  • It has 2 slots for RAM which means you can upgrade your RAM to 32 Gigabytes.
  • It also has an HDD slot, you can put SATA HDD or SSD in that slot, and can increase the storage of the laptop.

Cons
  • Battery backup is average
  • No Fingerprint
  • Only 2 USB Port
Link:HP Pavilion

2: ASUS TUF A15 (2021) Gaming, Business (AMD Ryzen 7 4800H) Laptop:

This laptop is a true beast guys, it comes with an octa-core processor. Though the gen is 4th and not the latest one, still it defeats most of the 5th gen processors, if you want my simple answer then yes it is better than Ryzen 5 5600H processor. If these generations don’t matter to you, then you can buy this laptop without thinking twice.

It comes with Ryzen 7 4800H (8 cores and 16 threads) processor. And it has a 144Hz 15.6-inch display. It has an 8GB Ram pre-installed on it, but you can upgrade the RAM to 32 Gigabytes according to your needs. And it has RTX 3050 GPU with 4GB VRAM.

Pros:
  • Ryzen 7 (8 core processor)
  • RTX 3050
  • 144 Hz Display
  • Ram upgradeable up to 32GB
  • Great upgradability options

Cons:
  • Ryzen 4th Gen Processor
  • Average battery backup
Link: ASUS TUF A15

3. Dell 15 (2021) Ryzen 5-5600H Gaming Laptop:

Dell 15 comes with Ryzen 5 5600H processor, and this laptop too has RTX 3050 in it, and it also has 8GB of RAM. It comes with Windows 11 pre-installed. It has a 15.6 inch Full HD display. The RAM can be upgraded to 16GB in the future. It comes with a 512GB SSD.

Pros:
    RTX 3050
  • Dell name is enough
  • Win 11 loaded
  • Latest Gen CPU

Cons:
  • Average battery backup
Link: Dell 15
